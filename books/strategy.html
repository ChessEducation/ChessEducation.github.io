Protecting Pieces Example

Protecting Pieces

To protect a piece, a player must put ones piece in anothers danger square. This adds a layer of defence on that piece, and on that file, rank, or square.

Battery Example

Battery

A battery is a formation of two or more pieces in which all the pieces protect each other. It is illegal for the King to capture a piece in a battery as the King would be placed in one of the opponent's danger squares. The most famous battery is Alekhine's Gun which consists of a connected rook pair on the same file or rank with a queen behind them.
