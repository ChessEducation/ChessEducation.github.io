What are Danger Squares?

Danger Squares are the squares on the board where a piece can capture. The King is unable to move into any of the opponent's danger squares as it would put him in check

What is a Check?

Check is when the King is put into one of the opponent's danger squares and he IS able to protect himself. When a King is put into check, he is forced to either move out of the danger square or put another one of his pieces in the attacker's way.

What is Checkmate?

Check mate is when the King is put into check but he IS NOT able to move to safety or block the attacker. Checkmate is one of the ways to win chess.
